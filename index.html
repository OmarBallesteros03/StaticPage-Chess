About Chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game that has been played for centuries. It is a game of skill, intelligence, and foresight, where each player controls an army of 16 pieces with the ultimate goal of checkmating the opponent's king. Chess is played on an 8x8 grid called a chessboard, which alternates between light and dark squares.
